# Math Accuracy Verification Reference

## Error Detection Categories

### CRITICAL - Mathematical Errors
- Incorrect formulas or equations
- Wrong calculations or arithmetic
- Incorrect definitions or theorems
- Logical fallacies in proofs
- Sign errors or coefficient mistakes

### HIGH - Contradictions
- Statements contradicting earlier definitions
- Inconsistent notation usage
- Examples contradicting stated rules

### MEDIUM - Ambiguities
- Unclear explanations that could be misinterpreted
- Missing assumptions or conditions
- Undefined variables or terms

### LOW - Notation/Pedagogical
- Variable naming inconsistencies
- Formatting inconsistencies
- Skipped steps in examples
- Missing intermediate calculations

## Evidence Requirements
For each issue:
1. Quote the exact problematic text or formula
2. Provide location (section/example number)
3. Explain WHY it's an error
4. Suggest a specific correction
